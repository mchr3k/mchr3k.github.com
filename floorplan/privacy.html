Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Floor Plan Designer (the “app”) is a free, browser-based tool for sketching room layouts. It is a static web page with no backend server: your data stays on your device, and — only if you choose to enable it — in your own Google Drive. We do not run analytics, we do not use tracking cookies, and we never sell or share your data.

What the app stores

  • On your device. Your floor plans, layouts and preferences are saved in your browser’s localStorage. This never leaves your device unless you enable Google Drive sync or use Export.
  • In your Google Drive (optional). If you connect Google Drive, the app stores a single floorplan.json file containing your plans, so you can sync between devices.

Google Drive access

Drive sync uses the least-privilege https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file scope. This means the app can only see and modify files it creates, or files you explicitly open with it — it cannot see, read, or access any other files in your Google Drive. Your Google sign-in token is held only in your browser and is used solely to read and write your plan file. You can disconnect at any time from the in-app Drive sync dialog, and revoke access at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Limited Use disclosure

Floor Plan Designer’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Data obtained through Google Drive is used only to provide the app’s sync feature, is processed entirely within your browser, is not transferred to any server, and is not used for advertising or any other purpose.

Sharing

The app does not transmit your data to us or to any third party. If you choose to share a Google Drive file with other people, that sharing is governed by Google Drive’s own permissions and privacy terms.

Deleting your data

  • Clear the app’s local data via your browser, or use Clear in the app.
  • Disconnect Drive in the app and delete the floorplan.json file from your Google Drive.
  • Revoke the app’s Google access at the link above.

Children

The app is a general-purpose tool and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact
